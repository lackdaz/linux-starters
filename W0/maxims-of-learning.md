> Beware of Mt. Stupid

```
~~~toilet -f future -t
Beware of Mt. Stupid
~~~
```

    Self-
    perception  ▲
    of           │    ╭╮  ← Mt. Stupid
    competence   │   ╭╯╰╮
                 │  ╭╯  ╰──╮              ╭─
                 │ ╭╯       ╰─────────────╯
                 │╭╯
                 ╰┼──────────────────────────▶
               Novice    Intermediate    Expert
                           Competence

Every learning journey starts with the Dunning-Kruger (DK) Effect

Learners have a 'double burden of incompetence':

1. Regular incompetence

1. The metacognitive inability to realize you are incompetent

The latter can affect your ability to learn effectively

Experts often understimate themselves and overestimate learning effort
